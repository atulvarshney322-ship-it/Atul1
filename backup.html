Project Highlights

Damac Islands

DAMAC Islands is an exclusive waterfront development located in Dubai, UAE, nestled in one of the city’s most sought-after coastal areas. This luxurious destination offers stunning views of the Arabian Gulf and is strategically positioned for easy access to major landmarks such as Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah, and JBR. With its close proximity to Sheikh Zayed Road, residents enjoy convenient access to Dubai’s key destinations, business districts, and leisure hubs.

DAMAC Properties, established in 2002, is a leading developer in the Middle East's luxury real estate sector. Renowned for delivering high-end residential, commercial, and leisure properties, the company has expanded its portfolio across key global markets, including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Jordan, Lebanon, the Maldives, Canada, and the United Kingdom. Known for its iconic and sophisticated developments, DAMAC has set a benchmark in the industry by creating opulent living spaces, world-class shopping destinations, and leisure facilities. The company has partnered with some of the most prestigious global brands, including Versace, Fendi, and Bugatti, to bring exceptional design and craftsmanship to its properties. With a commitment to quality and innovation, DAMAC has cemented its reputation as a leader in luxury real estate, continually pushing the boundaries of what modern living and luxury experiences should be.

The design of DAMAC Islands focuses on contemporary architecture, with a strong emphasis on luxurious waterfront living. The residences, including villas and townhouses, are crafted to showcase modern aesthetics and functionality, featuring expansive floor-to-ceiling windows that provide breathtaking, unobstructed sea views.
